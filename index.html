      Victoria's Resource Management Story

      Welcome to our interactive dashboard exploring Victoria's journey towards sustainable resource management. This visualization tool provides insights into the state's electricity and water consumption patterns across various suburbs and regions.

      By analyzing this data, we can identify areas of high consumption, track progress in conservation efforts, and make informed decisions for a more sustainable future. Explore the visualizations below to discover how different regions compare in their resource usage and learn about the factors influencing these patterns.
