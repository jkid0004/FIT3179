Victoria Energy Consumption, Production and Facilities by SA2 (2012)

The visualisation clearly shows how the different regions of Victoria produce and consume power for the rest of the state. You can additionally observe how the different facilities in Victoria produce and provide power to each other.

Water vs Electricity Consumption in Melbourne Suburbs by Region

By comparing the different Melbourne suburbs by region, you can see a clear divide between the regions alongside how the suburbs' water and electricity consumption completely contrast depending on their situation in Melbourne. Inner Melbourne has the highest energy consumption whilst Northern Melbourne has the highest water consumption.
